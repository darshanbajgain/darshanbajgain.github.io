Ways to be busy during lockdown

First off, I hope you are all keeping healthy and well. Who would have guessed that we would have been in this situation ?

1. Make most of your time productive. Don't simply waste your time in unproductive things. Use your time most for your future work. If you're a student,use your most of the time in learning newthings with great passion ad this will surely benefits you i future.
